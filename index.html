Latest Project

Spatially Processed TensorFlow Layer

Building on current research being done in spatially embedded recurrent neural networks used to study spatial dynamics found in the brain, I developed a simplified computationally efficient version aiming to determine if functionality defines shape or if shape defines functionality. I implemented the custom layer in a convolutional neural network and tested it in multiple configurations against the standard dense layer configurations, performed detailed analysis on the results to determine that spatial structure most likely arises because of physical limitations and not as a form of additional computation
